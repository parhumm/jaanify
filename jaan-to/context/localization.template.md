## RTL Handling

**RTL Languages**: FA (Persian)

**Special Requirements for Persian**:

### ZWNJ (Zero-Width Non-Joiner)

**What it is**: Invisible character (U+200C) used in Persian typography

**When to use**:
- **Plurals**: کاربرها‌ (users) - ZWNJ after pluralization suffix
- **Compound words**: نیمه‌تمام (half-finished) - ZWNJ between word parts
- **Keyboard shortcut**: Shift+Space (macOS), Ctrl+Shift+2 (Windows)

**Examples**:
- ✓ Correct: `کاربرها‌ی‌ما` (our users)
- ✗ Wrong: `کاربرهایما` (no ZWNJ)

### Punctuation

**Persian punctuation** (different from Latin):
- **Question mark**: ؟ (not ?)
- **Comma**: ، (not ,)
- **Semicolon**: ؛ (not ;)
- **Quotation marks**: « » (not " ")

**Example**:
- ✓ Persian: `آیا مطمئن هستید؟`
- ✗ Latin: `آیا مطمئن هستید?`

### Numeral Systems

**Three numeral systems**:
1. **Western numerals** (0123456789) - **Recommended for digital UI**
2. **Eastern Persian numerals** (۰۱۲۳۴۵۶۷۸۹) - Culturally authentic but font compatibility issues
3. **Arabic numerals** (٠١٢٣٤٥٦٧٨٩) - Used in some Arabic contexts

**Best practice**: Use Western numerals (0-9) for digital interfaces for consistency and compatibility.

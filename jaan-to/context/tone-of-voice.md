## Tone Profile {#profile}

### Core Attributes

| Attribute | Level | Description |
|-----------|-------|-------------|
| **Formality** | Informal-friendly | Casual but capable, like a helpful friend |
| **Warmth** | Warm | Reassuring, never cold or robotic |
| **Directness** | Direct | Say what it is, no fluff or corporate hedging |
| **Emotion** | Calm-positive | Gentle encouragement, not over-the-top celebration |

### Personality Traits

- **Human**: We talk like a supportive friend, not a corporate chatbot
- **Transparent**: We show our work — if AI does something, we say why
- **Calm**: We don't add urgency or anxiety — your tasks aren't emergencies
- **Respectful**: We respect attention as a scarce resource, especially for neurodivergent users

## Writing Principles {#principles}

### 1. Start with Action Verbs

✅ **Good**: "Choose your role to personalize your experience"
❌ **Bad**: "Your role can be selected here"

### 2. Celebrate Progress

✅ **Good**: "Great! You're all set to start learning"
❌ **Bad**: "Setup complete"

### 3. Be Specific, Not Vague

✅ **Good**: "We need notification access to remind you about your daily plan"
❌ **Bad**: "Notification access required"

### 4. Remove Blame Language

✅ **Good**: "Email format should be: name@example.com"
❌ **Bad**: "You entered an invalid email"

### 5. Encourage Next Steps

✅ **Good**: "Ready to plan your day? Let's organize your tasks"
❌ **Bad**: "Click here to continue"

## Tone Checklist {#checklist}

Use this checklist when writing microcopy:

- [ ] Does this sound encouraging rather than demanding?
- [ ] Have I removed blame language ("you forgot", "you failed")?
- [ ] Is the instruction specific and actionable?
- [ ] Does this celebrate progress when applicable?
- [ ] Have I used the formal pronoun consistently?
- [ ] Is the language warm but professional (not overly casual)?
- [ ] Does this guide the user to the next step?
- [ ] Is the reading level accessible (7-8th grade)?

## Anti-Patterns to Avoid {#anti-patterns}

### ❌ Overly Technical

**Bad**: "Authentication token expired. Re-authenticate to continue."
**Good**: "Your session has ended. Please sign in again to continue."

### ❌ Apologetic Overload

**Bad**: "We're so sorry, but unfortunately your payment could not be processed at this time."
**Good**: "Payment couldn't be processed. Please check your card details and try again."

### ❌ Vague Instructions

**Bad**: "Something went wrong."
**Good**: "Connection lost. Check your internet and tap Retry."

### ❌ Robot Language

**Bad**: "Operation successful. Click OK to proceed."
**Good**: "All done! You're ready to start planning your day."

## Cultural Adaptation Notes {#cultural}

### Persian (FA)
- Use warm, polite phrasing ("لطفاً" = please)
- Add motivational language ("عالی!" = excellent!)
- Respect formality with شما pronoun

### Russian (RU)
- Be direct and factual (less apologetic than English)
- Use Вы pronoun for respect
- Focus on solution, not problem

### German (DE)
- Be precise and clear
- Avoid overapologizing (not culturally expected)
- Use Sie pronoun for professionalism

### Turkish (TR)
- Be polite and respectful (Siz pronoun)
- Use motivational language ("Harika!" = great!)
- Expect longer text due to agglutinative grammar

### French (FR)
- Elegant phrasing preferred
- Vous pronoun for formality
- Use conditional for polite error messages ("pourriez-vous..." = could you...)
